What this means operationally
Bus 76 and 48 are your priority investments — already over thermal limit at baseline, pushed further by both scenarios. Upgrade or load-shedding schemes needed now.
Buses 65, 49, 47 are in the 60–63% range — not critical today, but a second heat event or accelerated EV growth tips them into the alert zone.
The ~60 buses in the 17–18% thermal band are well within safe limits under both 2027 scenarios. No near-term action needed there.
